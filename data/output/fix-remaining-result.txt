    all test imports. This should be a dedicated task with full TDD, not a side fix.

  RECOMMENDED NEXT STEP (for future work):
  - Create src/claude_bridge/commands/__init__.py
  - Move cmd_* groups into agent.py, task.py, team.py, setup.py
  - Keep cli.py as thin dispatcher importing from commands/
  - Update all test imports
  - Estimate: 2-3h with full test coverage
